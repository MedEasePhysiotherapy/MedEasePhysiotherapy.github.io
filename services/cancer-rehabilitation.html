Cancer Rehabilitation

Holistic Rehabilitation for Cancer Survivors

Cancer rehabilitation is designed to help cancer survivors regain strength, function, and quality of life during and after treatment. Our comprehensive programs address the physical, emotional, and psychological challenges that come with cancer treatment.

We provide personalized care that adapts to your changing needs throughout your cancer journey, helping you maintain independence and improve your overall well-being.

Treatment Phases

Pre-treatment (Prehabilitation)

  • Fitness assessment and conditioning
  • Strength and endurance training
  • Education about treatment side effects
  • Breathing and relaxation techniques

During Treatment

  • Fatigue management strategies
  • Gentle exercise programs
  • Pain management techniques
  • Maintaining mobility and function
  • Lymphedema prevention

Post-treatment Recovery

  • Gradual return to normal activities
  • Strength and conditioning programs
  • Scar tissue management
  • Bone health optimization
  • Neurological rehabilitation if needed

Common Issues We Address

  • Cancer-related fatigue
  • Muscle weakness and deconditioning
  • Lymphedema
  • Peripheral neuropathy
  • Balance and coordination problems
  • Bone density loss
  • Surgical site restrictions
  • Chemotherapy-induced symptoms
  • Radiation therapy side effects

Our Specialized Services

  • Lymphedema therapy and management
  • Scar tissue mobilization
  • Gentle exercise prescription
  • Balance and fall prevention training
  • Pain management techniques
  • Functional movement training
  • Breathing exercises and techniques
  • Stress management and relaxation

Benefits of Cancer Rehabilitation

  • Reduced treatment-related side effects
  • Improved physical function and strength
  • Enhanced quality of life
  • Faster recovery from treatment
  • Better management of fatigue
  • Increased confidence and independence
  • Improved mental health and mood
