Cardiac Rehabilitation

Empowering Your Recovery with Tailored Cardiopulmonary Care

Benefits

  • Improves cardiovascular endurance and functional capacity
  • Reduces risk of recurrent cardiac events and hospital readmissions
  • Optimizes heart rate, blood pressure, and exercise tolerance
  • Enhances quality of life and independence

Conditions We Treat

  • Myocardial Infarction (Heart Attack)
  • Post CABG / Bypass Surgery
  • Post Angioplasty (PTCA / Stenting)
  • Heart Failure (HFrEF / HFpEF)
  • Coronary Artery Disease (CAD)
  • Valvular Heart Diseases

What We Do

  • Baseline cardiac assessment (vitals, RPE, functional capacity like 6MWT)
  • Structured aerobic training (treadmill, walking, cycling)
  • Resistance training (phase-wise progression)
  • Risk factor management (diabetes, hypertension, obesity)
  • ECG-monitored / vitals-based exercise prescription (home-based monitoring)
  • Lifestyle modification and cardiac education
  • Progress tracking with reassessments and reports
