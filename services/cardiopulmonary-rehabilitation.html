Cardiopulmonary Rehabilitation

Empowering Your Recovery with Tailored Cardiopulmonary Care

Our specialized programs focus on helping patients recover from heart attacks, heart surgery, and lung conditions. We design personalized rehabilitation plans to improve cardiovascular function, respiratory efficiency, and overall endurance.

Dr. Nishtha Singhal holds a Master's Degree in cardiopulmonary physiotherapy, bringing specialized expertise to help you achieve optimal heart and lung health.

Conditions We Treat

  • Post-heart attack rehabilitation
  • Post-cardiac surgery recovery
  • Chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD)
  • Asthma management
  • Pneumonia recovery
  • Pulmonary fibrosis
  • Heart failure management
  • Coronary artery disease
  • Shortness of breath conditions

Our Treatment Methods

We use evidence-based techniques to improve your cardiovascular and respiratory health:

  • Supervised exercise training
  • Breathing techniques and exercises
  • Airway clearance techniques
  • Endurance and strength training
  • Patient education on lifestyle modifications
  • Monitoring of vital signs during exercise
  • Stress management techniques

Benefits of Cardiopulmonary Rehabilitation

  • Improved cardiovascular endurance
  • Better respiratory function
  • Reduced risk of future cardiac events
  • Enhanced quality of life
  • Increased confidence in daily activities
  • Better management of symptoms
