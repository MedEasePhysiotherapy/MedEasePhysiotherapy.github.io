Vestibular Rehabilitation

Comprehensive Care for Dizziness, Vertigo, and Imbalance

Vestibular rehabilitation is a specialized form of therapy designed to alleviate problems caused by vestibular disorders. Our comprehensive programs help patients overcome dizziness, vertigo, and balance issues through targeted exercises and techniques.

Our evidence-based approach helps retrain your brain to recognize and process signals from the vestibular system, reducing symptoms and improving your quality of life.

Conditions We Treat

  • Benign Paroxysmal Positional Vertigo (BPPV)
  • Vestibular neuritis
  • Labyrinthitis
  • Meniere's disease
  • Post-concussion syndrome
  • Vestibular migraine
  • Age-related balance disorders
  • Acoustic neuroma (post-surgical)
  • Chronic dizziness and imbalance

Treatment Techniques

  • Canalith repositioning procedures (Epley maneuver)
  • Gaze stabilization exercises
  • Balance training and postural stability
  • Habituation exercises
  • Substitution exercises
  • Fall prevention strategies
  • Visual tracking exercises
  • Proprioceptive training

Benefits of Vestibular Rehabilitation

  • Reduced dizziness and vertigo symptoms
  • Improved balance and coordination
  • Enhanced confidence in daily activities
  • Decreased risk of falls
  • Better quality of life
  • Reduced need for medication
  • Improved visual stability
