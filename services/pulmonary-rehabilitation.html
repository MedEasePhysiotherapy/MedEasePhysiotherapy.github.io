Pulmonary Rehabilitation

Comprehensive Care for Better Breathing and Lung Health

Benefits

  • Reduces breathlessness (dyspnea)
  • Improves lung function and oxygen utilization
  • Enhances exercise tolerance and daily activity performance
  • Reduces frequency of exacerbations

Conditions We Treat

  • Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease (COPD)
  • Interstitial Lung Disease (ILD)
  • Asthma
  • Bronchiectasis
  • Pulmonary Hypertension
  • Pre & Post Lung Surgery / Transplant
  • Post-COVID Lung Fibrosis

What We Do

  • Pulmonary function-based assessment (SpO₂, breathlessness scales)
  • Breathing retraining (diaphragmatic, pursed-lip breathing)
  • Airway clearance techniques (ACBT, postural drainage)
  • Aerobic conditioning with oxygen monitoring
  • Inspiratory muscle training
  • Energy conservation techniques
  • Patient education and flare-up management
