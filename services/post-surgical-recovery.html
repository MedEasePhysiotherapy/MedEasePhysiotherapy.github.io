Post-Surgical Recovery

Optimizing Recovery for a Stronger, Healthier You

Recovering from a surgery can be challenging. Our post-surgical recovery services aim to accelerate healing, restore function, and reduce the risk of complications through targeted physiotherapy.

We provide comprehensive rehabilitation programs tailored to your specific surgical procedure and recovery needs, helping you regain strength, mobility, and confidence.

Surgical Procedures We Support

  • Joint replacement surgeries (hip, knee, shoulder)
  • Arthroscopic procedures
  • Spinal surgeries
  • Cardiac surgeries
  • Abdominal surgeries
  • Orthopedic trauma surgeries
  • Sports injury surgeries
  • Reconstructive surgeries

Recovery Phases

Phase 1: Immediate Post-Surgery (0-2 weeks)

  • Pain management techniques
  • Gentle range of motion exercises
  • Wound care education
  • Breathing exercises
  • Basic mobility training

Phase 2: Early Recovery (2-6 weeks)

  • Progressive strengthening exercises
  • Improved range of motion
  • Functional movement training
  • Gait training if applicable
  • Scar tissue management

Phase 3: Advanced Recovery (6+ weeks)

  • Sport-specific or activity-specific training
  • Advanced strength and conditioning
  • Return-to-work preparation
  • Injury prevention education
  • Long-term maintenance planning

Our Approach

We work closely with your surgical team to ensure a coordinated approach to your recovery. Our evidence-based treatments are designed to optimize healing while minimizing complications.
