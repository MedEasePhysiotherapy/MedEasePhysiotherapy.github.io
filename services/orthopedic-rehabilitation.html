Orthopedic Rehabilitation

Restore Mobility, Relieve Pain, and Regain Strength with Specialized Orthopedic Care

Benefits

  • Reduces pain and inflammation
  • Restores mobility, strength, and function
  • Prevents recurrence and chronic disability
  • Enhances return to daily and occupational activities

Conditions We Treat

  • Back Pain, Neck Pain
  • Osteoarthritis, Rheumatoid Arthritis
  • Post-fracture and post-surgical rehab
  • Ligament injuries (ACL, meniscus, etc.)
  • Shoulder, knee, and hip dysfunctions

What We Do

  • Musculoskeletal assessment (ROM, strength, special tests)
  • Pain management techniques
  • Manual therapy and mobility restoration
  • Strengthening and stability training
  • Posture correction and ergonomic advice
  • Functional and return-to-activity training
