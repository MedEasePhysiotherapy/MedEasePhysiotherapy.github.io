Dr. Nishtha Singhal

Master of Physiotherapy

Specialist in Cardiopulmonary Physiotherapy

Dr. Nishtha Singhal (BPT, MPT – Cardiopulmonary Physiotherapy, MAHE) is the Founder and Director of MedEase Physiotherapy, dedicated to delivering specialized, evidence-based rehabilitation services.

Based in Mumbai, she actively serves patients across Borivali to Andheri through home visits, along with pan-India tele-rehabilitation services.

She specializes in Cardiopulmonary Physiotherapy, with focused expertise in:

  • Cardiac Rehabilitation — Post CABG, Angioplasty, Heart Failure
  • Pulmonary Rehabilitation — COPD, ILD, Post-COVID, Chronic Lung Diseases
  • Oncology Rehabilitation
  • Lifestyle Disorder Management — Obesity, Diabetes, Hypertension

Dr. Nishtha has contributed to research in the field of oncology rehabilitation, having worked on a research publication at KMC Mangalore, MAHE, under the guidance of Dr. Stephen Samuel and Dr. Vijaya Kumar.

Her clinical experience includes working across both Intensive Care Units (ICU) and Outpatient Departments (OPD), giving her strong competence in managing patients across acute, subacute, and recovery phases.

In addition, she has extensive experience in delivering home-based physiotherapy and tele-rehabilitation, ensuring continuity of care beyond hospital settings with structured, monitored, and patient-specific rehabilitation programs.

Treatment Philosophy

My approach to physiotherapy is centered on evidence-based practice combined with compassionate, patient-centered care. I believe in empowering patients with knowledge and skills to take an active role in their recovery journey.

I work collaboratively with each patient to develop personalized treatment plans that address their unique needs, goals, and lifestyle. My focus is not just on treating symptoms, but on addressing the root causes of dysfunction and promoting long-term health and wellness.

Professional Experience

With extensive experience in cardiopulmonary physiotherapy, I have helped numerous patients recover from heart attacks, cardiac surgeries, and various pulmonary conditions. My expertise extends to working with patients across the entire spectrum of cardiovascular and respiratory health.

I stay current with the latest research and treatment techniques through continuous professional development, ensuring that my patients receive the most effective and up-to-date care available.
