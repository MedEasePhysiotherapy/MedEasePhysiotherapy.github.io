Therapist

Dr. Nishtha Singhal

Master of Physiotherapy

Dr. Nishtha Singhal (BPT, MPT – Cardiopulmonary Physiotherapy, MAHE) is a Mumbai-based physiotherapist with clinical experience across hospitals including BSES MG Hospital, Zynova Shalby Hospital, and Sir H. N. Reliance Foundation Hospital. She specializes in evidence-based cardiac, pulmonary, oncology, and lifestyle disorder rehabilitation. Her expertise includes post-CABG and angioplasty rehabilitation, COPD, ILD, obesity, diabetes, and hypertension management. With experience in both ICU and OPD settings, she provides structured home visits across Borivali to Andheri and tele-rehabilitation services across India, focusing on personalized care, functional recovery, patient education, and long-term health improvement.
